About Us

We are a recycling company based in Dakata, Kano State. With over 5 years of expertise in HDPE recycling, we have recently expanded into PET bottle sorting and baling. We serve local and international recycling plants by delivering clean PET feedstock for rPET production.

HDPE Recycling

We process HDPE plastic waste into reusable industrial material for manufacturers.

PET Bottle Sorting

Our team ensures top-quality sorting of PET bottles, preparing them for food-grade recycling.

Supply of Baled PET

We ship baled PET to local and international clients, meeting rPET-grade specifications.
